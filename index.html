The Imposter Party Game

Works locally or over the internet. No account needed.

HOW TO PLAY

3–8 players

1
Everyone joins a room. The host picks a topic category and starts the game.
2
Each player privately sees their role: Crewmate sees the secret topic. The Imposter doesn't.
3
During discussion, everyone chats. Crewmates give clues about the topic. Imposter bluffs!
4
After discussion, everyone votes for who they think the Imposter is.
5
Crewmates win if they vote out the Imposter. Imposter wins if they survive!
